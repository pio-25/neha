THE CHALLENGE

One building, three disciplines,
zero tolerance

A clash between a structural beam and an MEP duct can cost tens of thousands in rework. This project developed a comprehensive federated BIM model then ran systematic clash detection across three platforms to understand the capabilities and limits of each.

Navisworks
Visual and automated clash detection
Same and cross-discipline analysis
Issue assignment and responsibility
Detailed clash analysis reports
Solibri
Customised role and rule sets
Model checking beyond geometry
Severity classification of clashes
Structured issue tracking
Autodesk CC
Cloud-based automated detection
Cross-discipline coordination
Issue assignment with notifications
Integrated BIM 360 ecosystem
