ISO 19650 BIM Strategy

Strategic BIM Adoption Plan
AEC Firm Implementation

ISO 19650 workflows, CDE setup, BEP development, responsibility matrices, risk assessment, information exchange protocols, and performance monitoring — built as a replicable model for future project delivery.

STANDARDS
ISO 19650 · RIBA · Uniclass 2015 · NBS BIM Toolkit
TOOLS
Revit · BIM 360 · ISO 19650 Framework
THE CHALLENGE

An AEC firm that needed a digital backbone

Not just adopting tools — building a replicable framework for every future project. Unified BIM processes, an upskilled project team, alignment with UK BIM mandates. The goal was a BEP that people would actually use.


Collaboration across AEC
Interdisciplinary communication strategies, effective team integration, mutual understanding across architecture, engineering, and construction disciplines.
Standards made operational
ISO 19650, RIBA Plan of Work, Integrated Project Delivery, Uniclass 2015, and NBS BIM Toolkit, implemented and cross-referenced throughout the BEP.
BIM process in detail
Naming conventions, CDE protocols, Level of Information, Level of Development, clash detection strategies, deliverable timelines — each documented and owned.
Performance monitoring
Dynamic frameworks adaptable across design, construction, and maintenance phases — continuous improvement, not a static document.
